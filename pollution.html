Inside Info of Air Pollution

Composition of Air

Air is composed of 78% nitrogran, 21% oxygen, 0.9% argon. The remaining composition include carbon dioxide, water vapor, hydrogen and many other traces of elements.

Types of air Pollutants

Stay away from particulate matter such as Carbon monoxide, ozone, nitrogen dioxide and suplhur dioxide.

Causes

Air pollution is caused by the combination of gaseous and particulate pollutants such as carbon dioxide, methane, and nitrogen dioxide emitted from sources such as factories and motor vehicles through combustion and burning of fuel.
