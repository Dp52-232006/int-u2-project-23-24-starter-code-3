Air Pollution and the Significant Impacts

What is Air Pollution?

Air pollution is the contamination of the air by any chemical, physical, or biological agent that affects the air quality indoor or outdoor. It can harm any living organism such as humans, animals, and plants

Why is it a Problem?

Air pollution is everywhere and so it is big part of our lives. It affects our health and environment in various ways some of which can cause diseases and even death.
